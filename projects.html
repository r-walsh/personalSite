The First Project

My first project was based (extremely) loosely on EA's Dragon Age Keep. Put simply, this website allows you to adjust decisions made throughout the Dragon Age series and validate your choices based on those selected previously.

While the scope of the website was beyond my skill, time, and resources I was still inspired to attempt something loosely similar. The final iteration ended up being a simple color matching game. The player is given 9 squares of 3 different colors and a target color they must match. If the player attempts to select two different colors, or matches three colors that are not the target color, the game will reset.

Validation of selections was done by comparing background-colors to the target-color and previous selections. This served as my first foray into jQuery, which I found significantly more convenient to use when selecting and manipulating DOM elements than vanilla Javascript.

A link to the game can be found on Codepen or GitHub. Thanks to Sean Walsh for suggestions and review.
